CR Number: 9,072
Type: Defect
Addressed In: 13.10.03 V8.1.0.005
Synopsis for Users: The Input Output Reference definition of Fan Efficiency may be confusing. For the simple fan models, the input for "Fan Efficiency" is the ratio of the power delivered to the air to the electrical input power at maximum flow.
Further Details for Users: This is also know at "Total Efficiency" which is the product of the fan motor efficiency, belt drive efficiency (if applicable), and the fan impeller efficiency.
